Frequently Asked Questions

Need help? We got you. Here are some common questions about Virtual Study Room.

What is the Virtual Study Room?

It's an online platform designed to help you focus, track your progress, and stay consistent with your studies. Includes tools like flashcards, Pomodoro timers, and more.

Is my data saved securely?

Yes. We store your data securely using best practices, and your login info is encrypted using PHP’s password hashing.

Can I use it without an account?

Some features like the Pomodoro timer may be usable without an account, but for full functionality — such as saving progress — you'll need to sign up.

Do you plan to add group study or chat features?

Yes! We're currently working on adding real-time rooms where users can join and study together, along with chat and accountability features.

Is it free?

Yes, all current features are completely free. We may offer optional upgrades or premium features in the future.
